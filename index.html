About Us

Our team was founded with a mission to address the growing opioid crisis in Portland, Oregon. Our care is guided by the voices of our clients and the cultural preferences of everyone involved. We are staffed by a diverse group of professionals who are licensed and specialize in chemical dependency and mental health. Our team is dedicated to reducing disparities and tackling the historical roots of substance abuse.

Our Services

  • Included in IOP
  • On-Site Treatment (3 to 5 days per Week)
  • Regular Drug & Alcohol Screening
  • Counseling and Therapy
  • On-site Medical Staff
  • Group Therapy
  • Family Therapy
  • Evenings Group
